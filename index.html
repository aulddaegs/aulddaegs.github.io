THE SAVAGE COMPUTERS

A Novel of the Contrapresent by Chris Pang


Life hurts. You know something’s wrong, but you don’t know what. The strikebreaker drone arcs through the sky, steadies, and prepares to dive. I’m standing in front of a grave. A mess of 3d polygons, a glitching model, a poorly rendered substitute, a grave. A room is a world.

It's time to save the world.
